Abstract

For my work term 3 I stayed at Magnet Forensics, where I also worked during work term 2. My decision to stay was mainly because of the great work culture, but also because the project I worked on spanned many phases. Magnet Forensics

Magnet Forensics is the leading provider of digital forensics products. They have played a big part in SaaS through their all-in-one platform, Magnet One. Moreover, their array of products has been used by over 90 countries around the globe, helping to fight crime and serve justice.

  • Help improve regression suite performance by 15% ‚ö°

    This work term was essentially a sequel to my work term 2, and so I had opportunities to improve the tool that I built, specifically improving performance. Many times in development, the initial solution is not always the most efficient, and hence there is often constant refactoring throughout the development cycles. For me, I focused on improving run-time performance by using certain techniques and redefining application behavior. For example, by using hashing techniques on specific content data, I was able to avoid loading actual content data into the app and only consider the hashes, which drastically improved performance. Ultimately, I achieved a 15% improvement in performance by the end of my term.
